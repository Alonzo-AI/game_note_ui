## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- With its first win of the season, UTSA improved to 1-2. UIW fell to 1-2.

UTSA now leads the series, 2-0.

The Roadrunners have won 18 of their last 20 home games.

UTSA is 38-25 versus teams from the state of Texas.

O UTSA is 10-0 overall against FCS teams.

- Head coach Jeff Traylor is 47-22 (.681) overall.

UTSA is 30-4 (.882) at the Alamodome in the Jeff Traylor era.

He is 18-9 versus teams from the state of Texas.

He is 5-0 against FCS teams.

## TEAM NOTES

- UTSA piled up 442 yards of total offense, with 243 passing and 199 rushing.

- The Roadrunners have now scored 20-or-more points in 13 straight games.

- The Roadrunners scored on their first possession for the first time this season, also marking the first time they scored first in a game this year.

- UTSA held UIW to 33 yards rushing, the fewest allowed since holding Rice to 5 yards on Nov.19,2022.

- The Roadrunners recorded 4 takeaways today, 2 fumble recoveries and a pair of interceptions.

The 4 takeaways are tied for the third most in a game in school history.

UTSA now has 5 takeaways this season, 2 fumble recoveries and 3 INTs.

- UTSA now has registered both a takeaway and a sack in 25 of the past 26 games.

- UTSA was a perfect 5-for-5 in the red zone.

- The Roadrunners shut out the Cardinals in the first half, marking the first time they've held an opponent scoreless in the opening half since Dec. 23, 2024 against Coastal Carolina in the Myrtle Beach Bowl.

## INDIVIDUAL NOTES

- Senior RB Robert Henry Jr. rushed for 144 yards and 2 touchdowns on 14 carries, an average of 10.3 yards per carry.

He now boasts at least 144 rushing yards, 2 rushing TDs and a rush of at least 74 yards in 5 consecutive games.

He is the first UTSA player to rush for 100-or-more yards in 5 consecutive games.

He has piled up 826 rushing yards and 11 total TDs (10 rushing) over his last 5 games,the most of any Roadrunner in that many consecutive games.

Henry now ranks sixth in program history with 1,774 career rushing yards.

- Junior QB Owen McCown threw for 243 yards and 4 touchdowns on 30-for-36 passing.

McCown tossed 4 touchdowns for the first time since besting No.25 Memphis on Nov.2,2024.

He completed 11 consecutive passes during the first half, a new UTSA career best and two shy of the program record. His previous best was 10 straight at Tennessee in 2023.

McCown logged a 40-yard punt placed inside the 20.

- Junior WR AJ Wilson caught 5 passes for 104 yards and a touchdown.

His 77-yard TD reception in the third quarter was UTSA's first TD catch of 70-plus yards since Oct.28,2023,and it is tied for the third-longest passing play in program history.

- Junior WR Devin McCuin hauled in 7 receptions for 51 yards and 2 scores.

He recorded 58 yards after the catch.

- Senior ILB Shad Banks Jr. registered 5 tackles, recorded a fumble recovery and snagged an interception for the second time in as many games.

Banks Jr. becomes the fourth Roadrunner to have a fumble recovery and an interception in the same game, and the first one since Triston Wade accomplished the feat on Oct.1,2014, against FIU.

ne last time a Roadrunner recorded multiple takeaways in a single game was Zah Fraizer with 2 interceptions against Temple on Nov. 22, 2024.

- Sophomore CB Davin Martin posted 6 tackles and his first career interception.

- Sophomore S Jimmy Wyrick tallied 4 tackles and a fumble recovery.

- Junior LB Brandon Tucker topped the team with 8 total tackles, 7 of which were solo.

- Junior CB KK Meier turned in 5 tackles.

- Senior P Caile Hogan averaged 47.3 yards on 3 punts, with 2 inside the 20 and a long of 53 yards.

- Sophomore PK Michael Petro was good on his 2 field goal attempts, splitting the uprights from 26 and 31 yards to make him 3-for-4 this season.
