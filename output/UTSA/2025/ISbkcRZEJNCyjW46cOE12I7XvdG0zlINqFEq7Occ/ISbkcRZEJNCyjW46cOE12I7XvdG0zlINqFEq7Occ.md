## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- UTSA improved to 6-5 overall and 4-3 in American Conference play. ECU fell to 7-4 and 5-2.

- This marked the third meeting and UTSA now leads the series, 2-1.

- UTSA is bowl eligible for the sixth straight season and seventh time overall.

- The Roadrunners are now 59-31 (.656) in home games.

- o UTSA has won 21 of its last 23 games at the Alamodome.

- UTSA is now 63-45 (.583) in conference games.

- The Roadrunners are 15-8 (.652) in American Conference games.

- Head coach Jeff Traylor is 52-25 (.675) overall (since 2020).

He is 33-4 (.892) in home games.

He is 25-0 against conference opponents at the Alamodome.

He is 35-11 (.761) in conference games including 15-8 (.652) in the American Conference.

He is 18-4 (.818) in the month of November.

UTSA has been bowl eligible in each of Traylor's six seasons.

## INDIVIDUAL NOTES

- Junior QB Owen McCown completed 24 of 33 passes for 248 yards with five touchdowns and no interceptions.

His 25 TD passes this season tie his total in 2024 for the third-most on UTSA's single-season list and are six shy of the record of 32 set by Frank Harris in 2022.

McCown becomes the first Roadrunner to toss five touchdown passes since Harris recorded a school-record six against Western Kentucky on Oct. 9, 2021.

- Redshirt freshman Will Henderson III racked up 146 rushing yards.

It marked his second straight and third overall 100-yard game of the season.

After posting 185 last week vs. Charlotte, he has racked up 332 yards rushing over the past two games.

- Sophomore TE Patrick Overmyer tallied three receptions for 58 yards.
