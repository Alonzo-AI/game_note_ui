## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- UTSA improved to 2-2. Colorado State fell to 1-2.

Colorado State leads the series, 2-1.

- UTSA is now 8-7 in the Mountain time zone.

- The Roadrunners are now 36-49 (.424) in road games.

- Head coach Jeff Traylor is 48-22 (.685) overall.

- The Roadrunners are now 26-14 (.650) under Jeff Traylor in games decided by one score, and 15-8 (.652) in games decided by eight points or less since 2021.

## TEAM NOTES

- UTSA had a balanced attack, with 173 passing yards and 172 rushing yards accounting for 345 yards of total offense

- The Roadrunners recorded their first one-point victory since taking down Louisiana Tech, 27-26 on Oct. 24, 2020.

- UTSA now has registered a sack in 25 of the past 26 games.

## INDIVIDUAL NOTES

- Senior RB Robert Henry Jr. rushed for 144 yards on 21 carries and caught 2 passes for 76 yards, recording both a rushing and a receiving touchdown.

He totaled 220 all-purpose yards for the ninth-best single-game total in program history,and the most since he charted 225 against North Texas on Nov.15,2024

His season-long 76-yard TD dash in the second quarter marked his fourth score on the ground of 74-or-more yards this year.

He now boasts at least 144 rushing yards,2 or more total TDs and a rush of at least 74 yards in 6 consecutive games.

He is the first UTSA player to rush for 100-or-more yards in 6 consecutive games.

He has piled up 970 rushing yards and 13 total TDs (11 rushing) over his last6 games,the most of any Roadrunner in that many consecutive games.

Henry now ranks fifth in program history with 1,918 career rushing yards, as he surpassed Kevorian Barnes (1,797) tonight.

- Henry racked up 76 total receiving yards for a career-high, which included a 74-yard touchdown reception, also a career high.

- Junior QB Owen McCown completed 14-of-27 passes for 173 yards and a touchdown.

- Junior WR Devin McCuin hauled in four receptions for 50 yards.

Sophomore OLB Nnanna Anyanwu posted five tackles - four for loss - with three sacks and a pass breakup.

He is the seventh Roadrunner to record three or more sacks in a game and the first since Jimmori Robinson had a school record-tying four against Temple on Nov. 22, 2024.

He also became the sixth UTSA player to log four or more tackles for loss in a game and the first since Robinson's 5.5 against Temple

He earned his first start as a Roadrunner and registered his first sack on the first series of the game.

- Senior ILB Shad Banks Jr. and redshirt sophomore S Jimmy Wyrick each tallied 11 tackles, marking the first time that multiple Roadrunners logged double-digit tackles since Dadrian Taylor and Jamal Ligon each had 10 on Sept. 3, 2022, against Houston.

Banks Jr. leads UTSA with 31 tackles this season, and his 11 tackles tonight tied his career high.

11 tackles represent a new career-high for Wyrick.

- Senior S Jermarius Lewis posted eight tackles.

- Redshirt Junior $B Bryce Grays logged a career-best six tackles

- Sophomore PK Michael Petro drilled a career-long 40-yard field goal late in the first half and was 2-for-2 on point after attempts.

- Senior P Caile Hogan averaged 44.5 yards on six punts with 2 inside the 20 and a long of 51 yards.
