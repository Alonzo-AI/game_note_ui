## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- UTSA fell to 6-6 overall and 4-4 in American Conference play. Army improved to 6-5 and 4-4.

- This marked the sixth meeting and Army leads the series, 5-1.

- The Roadrunners are now 59-32 (.648) in home games.

- UTSA is now 63-46 (.578) in conference games.

The Roadrunners are 15-9 (.625) in American Conference games.

- Head coach Jeff Traylor is 52-26 (.667) overall (since 2020).

He is 33-5 (.868) in home games.

He is 25-1 (.962) against conference opponents at the Alamodome.

He is 35-12 (.745) in conference games including 15-9 (.625) in the American Conference.

He is 18-5 (.783) in the month of November.

## TEAM NOTES

- UTSA posted 386 yards of total offense,270 through the air and 116 on the ground.

- The Roadrunners now have scored on their first possession in seven of the last eight games.

- The Roadrunners registered a sack and now have a sack in 34 of their last 35 games.

- UTSA held Army to 70 passing yards, marking the second time in 2025 that they've held an opponent under 100 after Rice recorded 79 on Oct. 10.

It is the fewest amount of passing yards allowed since Southern Miss managed 52 on Nov. 13, 2021.
