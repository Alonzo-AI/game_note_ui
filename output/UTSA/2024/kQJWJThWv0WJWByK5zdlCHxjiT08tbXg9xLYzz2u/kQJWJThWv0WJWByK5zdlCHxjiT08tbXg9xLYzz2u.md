## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- UTSA improved to 2-2 on the season with today's win. HCU fell to 1-3.

- This marked the first meeting between UTSA and HCU.

- The Roadrunners have won 10 of their last 13 games.

- UTSA has won 13 of its last 14 home games and 23 of the last 25 contests played at the Alamodome.

- The Roadrunners are 51-30 all-time at the Alamodome.

- With today's win over HCU, UTSA improved to:

41-16 (.719) under fifth-year head coach Jeff Traylor.

25-3 (.893) at the Alamodome in the Jeff Traylor era.

9-0 versus FCS teams as an FBS program.

36-22 all-time and 16-6 under Jeff Traylor against teams from the state of Texas.

- Today's attendance was 20,973.

## TEAM NOTES

- UTSA piled up a season-high 536 total yards of offense, 269 rushing and 267 passing.

The 536 yards represents the 11th-highest single-game total in school history.

The total surpassed the previous season best of 416 against Kennesaw State in the opener.

This marks the first time going over 500 yards of total offense since recording a school-record 643 vs. South Florida on Nov.17,2023.

The 269 rushing yards is the most since rushing for 287 at North Texas on Nov. 4, 2023.

- UTSA registered a school-record 34 first downs.

The previous school record was 32, done five times and most recently in the Nov.17,2023 win over South Florida.

- UTSA scored six total touchdowns, four in the air and two on the ground.

The six touchdowns are tied for the 10th-best single game performance, and represent the most since North Texas on Dec.2,2022.

The Roadrunners scored a season-high 45 points in the win over HCU.

The Roadrunners surpassed the previous season-best point total of 28 before halftime.

Today marked the most points scored by UTSA since scoring 49 against South Florida last season.

- UTSA scored on its opening possession for the second time this season.

The Roadrunners are 2-0 when scoring on their first drive.

- UTSA held HCU to seven points, the lowest of any opponent since Rice scored seven on Nov. 17, 2022.

- The Roadrunners held HCU to 177 yards of total offense,50 on the ground and 127 through the air.

UTSA has now held opponents under 100 yards rushing twice this year, previously against Kennesaw State (51 yards).

The season-low 50 yards rushing allowed was the fewest since Rice recorded 47 yards on Nov. 11, 2023.

- The Roadrunners extended their takeaway streak to 14 consecutive games with Jimmori Robinson's fumble recovery in the first quarter.

## INDIVIDUAL NOTES

- Sophomore QB Owen McCown completed 18 of 25 passes for 226 yards and three touchdowns.

McCown now has thrown for 803 yards and six TDs against zero interceptions on 77-of-115 passing this season.

The 59-yard TD reception is the longest scoring play of the season.

- Sophomore WR Devin McCuin caught five passes for 43 yards and a TD.

- Senior WR Chris Carpenter had 29 receiving yards on three catches.

- Redshirt freshman WR Jamel Hardy recorded his first career TD on his first catch as a Roadrunner with a 5-yard, one-handed grab in the fourth quarter.

- Junior RB Kevorian Barnes rushed for 64 yards on 12 attempts.

- Redshirt freshman RB Brandon High went for 74 yards and his first career TD on nine carries.

- Sophomore RB Bryson Donnell charted 74 yards on 12 carries, both career highs.

- Senior RB Robert Henry Jr. collected 53 all-purpose yards, with a receiving touchdown.

His blocked field goal was the first for UTSA since Tariq Woolen vs. Marshall on Nov. 17, 2018.
