## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- Today's game marked the sixth meeting between UTSA and Texas State.

- The Roadrunners still hold a 5-1 advantage in the all-time series with the Bobcats.

- UTSA is now 40-15 (.727) under fifth-year head coach Jeff Traylor.

- The Roadrunners are now 7-7 overall in road openers, 3-2 in the Jeff Traylor era.

- UTSA is now 35-21 (15-5 under Traylor) all-time against teams from the state of Texas.

- The Roadrunners are now 9-2 over their last 11 games.
