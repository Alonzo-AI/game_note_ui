# 2024 UTSA Roadrunners Football UTSA vs. Tulsa Saturday, Oct. 26 H.A. Chapman Stadium·Tulsa, Okla. UTSA Postgame Notes

## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- UTSA fell to 3-5 on the season and 1-3 in the American Athletic Conference. Tulsa improved to 3-5 overall, 1-3 in league play.

- This marked the second meeting between UTSA and Tulsa.

The teams are tied 1-1 in the all-time series

42-19 (.688) overall.

28-7 (.800) in regular-season conference games.

30-7 (.810) versus conference competition when including the 2021 and 2022 Conference USA Championship Games.

## TEAM NOTES

- The Roadrunners recorded 443 passing yards, a figure that serves as the new program record.

The previous benchmark of 441 came against Texas Southern on Sept. 24, 2022.

- UTSA's 546 yards of total offense represent a new season-high, surpassing the previous total of 536 against Houston Christian (9/21/24).

- Chris Carpenter (115) and Houston Thomas (132) both topped 100 yards receiving to mark the first time UTSA has had two 100-yard receivers in a game since Tykee Ogle-Kellogg (142) and Zakhari Franklin (126) against UTEP (11/26/22).

The pair logged the first 100-yard receiving game since Joshua Cephus had 102 in the 35-17 victory over Marshall in the Frisco Bowl (12/19/23)

- Today marked back-to-back games with a touchdown catch by multiple tight ends as Oscar Cardenas and Dan Dishman each found the end zone against Tulsa.

- UTSA extended its streak of consecutive games with a takeaway to 18.

- UTSA posted 15 tackles for loss, breaking the school record which had occurred four times before.

O It marks the second straight and fourth overall game with 10-or-more TFLs

- The Roadrunners registered a school record-tying seven sacks for the second straight game and now have recorded one in 18 straight games.

## INDIVIDUAL NOTES

- Redshirt sophomore QB Owen McCown set a new school record with 434 passing yards, going 30-for-50 with four touchdowns.

The previous record was held by Frank Harris, who charted 414 yards against Middle Tennessee

McCown's four passing touchdowns also represent the most in his career and tie multiple Harris performances for the second-most by a Roadrunner in a game.

- Senior WR Chris Carpenter hauled in seven passes for a career-high 115 yards and a score.

His 69-yard touchdown reception was UTSA's longest play of the season.

- Redshirt sophomore TE Houston Thomas caught six passes for a career-best 132 yards.

- Senior WR Willie McCoy recorded four receptions for 76 yards including a 49-yard catch.

- Senior RB Robert Henry Jr. posted 67 yards and a touchdown on 20 carres.

- Senior TE Oscar Cardenas caught a 9-yard touchdown pass in the first quarter.

Cardenas now has the most career touchdown receptions (9) by a UTSA tight end in addition to the most catches (95) and receiving yards (1,138) at the position in program history.

- Redshirt junior TE Dan Dishman caught a 23-yard pass for a touchdown in the second quarter.

- Senior ILB Martavius French posted a team-high eight tackles including 1.5 for loss.

- Senior DL Asyrus Simon registered six tackles, a team-high three TFLs and a sack.

- Senior LB Jamal Ligon had four tackles, a sack, a pass breakup, a forced fumble and a fumble recovery.

- Redshirt junior DL Ronald Triplette logged four tackles with two sacks and a forced fumble.

- Redshirt senior OLB Jimmori Robinson tallied 1.5 sacks and a forced fumble.

- Redshirt freshman DL Daemian Wimberly had one tackle and a 28-yard fumble return in the second quarter.

That marked UTSA's first fumble return for a touchdown since Lorenzo Dantzler's 3-yard return versus Middle Tennessee (9/18/21).

- Freshman P Caile Hogan recorded a career-long 63-yard punt in the fourth quarter,the first UTSA punt to go 60-plus yards since Lucas Dean booted one 67 yards against Troy in the Cure Bowl on Dec.16.2022.
