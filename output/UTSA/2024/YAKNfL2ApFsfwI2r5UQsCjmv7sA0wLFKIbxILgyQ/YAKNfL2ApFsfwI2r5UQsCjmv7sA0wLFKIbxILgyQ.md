## TEAM RECORDS AND SERIES NOTES

- UTSA kicked off its 14th season of football with today's game.

- This marked the first meeting between UTSA and Kennesaw State.

- With today's 28-16 win over Kennesaw State, UTSA improved to:

40-14 under fifth-year head coach Jeff Traylor.

24-3 (.889) at the Alamodome in the Jeff Traylor era.

10-4 in season openers

9-5 in home openers, including 4-1 under Jeff Traylor

- UTSA earned its 50th all-time win in the Alamodome and now owns a 50-30 record.

- Today's attendance was 25,911.

UTSA surpassed two million fans at home games in its history with today's attendance and now stands at 2,014,342 all-time.

## TEAM NOTES

- UTSA tallied 416 yards of offense,340 through the air and 76 on the ground.

- The Roadrunners held Kennesaw State to 253 yards of offense, including only 51 rushing, the fewest rushing yards allowed since limited Rice to 47 on Nov. 11, 2023.

- UTSA registered 10 tackles for loss, including three sacks.

The 10 TFLs are tied for 10th on UTSA's single-game chart.

Today was the first double-digit TFL game since 10 versus Rice game on Nov.11,2023.

## INDIVIDUAL NOTES

- Sophomore WR Devin McCuin had a career-high 11 receptions for 79 yards and a career-best two touchdowns.

His 11 receptions are tied for the second-most in a game by a Roadrunner

McCulin's 11 catches are most since Joshua Cephus caught 11 in the win over North Texas in the 2022 Conference USA Championship Game.

- Senior TE Oscar Cardenas became the ninth Roadrunner — and just the second tight end after David Morgan II (1,104)— to surpass 1,000 career receiving yards after his 35-yard catch late in the second quarter.

Cardenas also had a 3-yard TD reception in the fourth quarter.

- Sophomore QB Owen McCown threw for 340 yards and three touchdowns on 28-of-38 passing, including a UTSA career-long 61-yard third-down connection to Robert Henry at the start of the second quarter.

That passing yardage total now ranks 10th on UTSA's single-game list.

His passing output marks the third time a Roadrunner QB has thrown for 300-plus yards in a season opener, joining Blake Bogenschutz (332 at Arizona, 9/3/15) and Frank Harris (337 vs. Houston, 9/3/22)

His 28 completions also mark a career high.

McCown's three touchdown passes against the Owls marked the first 3-TD night for a UTSA passer since Frank Harris against South Florida on Nov.17,2023.

- Senior S Ken Robinson posted a team-high seven tackles including one tackle for loss.

- Sophomore $B Owen Pewee registered six tackles with one TFL and a pass breakup.

- Senior S Elliott Davison made five stops and broke up a pass.

- Senior OLB Jimmori Robinson, senior ILB Martavius French and redshirt freshman OLB James Walley Jr. each recorded a sack.

- Sophomore S Jimmy Wyrick forced a fumble that was recovered by senior CB Syrus Dumas.

## ADDITIONAL NOTES
